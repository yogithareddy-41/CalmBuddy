CalmBuddy: 24/7 Campus Wellness Companion
1. Introduction
CalmBuddy is a student-focused mental wellness chatbot designed to provide immediate emotional support, stress management tools, and academic guidance. The platform aims to create a safe, accessible, and stigma-free digital space where students can seek help anytime.
2. Problem Statement
College students face high levels of stress due to academic pressure, social expectations, career uncertainty, and personal challenges. Many students hesitate to seek professional help due to stigma, lack of awareness, or limited access to campus counseling services. CalmBuddy addresses this gap by offering instant, confidential, and structured support.
3. Objectives
• Provide 24/7 emotional support.
• Offer structured stress-relief tools.
• Assist with study planning and academic anxiety.
• Share verified helpline information when needed.
• Promote self-awareness and healthy coping mechanisms.
4. Key Features
1. Mood Selection Buttons (Anxious, Stressed, Need Study Help, Help Me Calm Down).
2. Guided grounding techniques and sensory reset exercises.
3. Study support with structured 25-minute focus plans.
4. Motivational cognitive reframing dialogues.
5. Journaling prompts for emotional clarity.
6. Emergency helpline integration for crisis situations.
5. User Interaction Flow
Users begin by selecting how they feel. Based on their choice, CalmBuddy provides tailored dialogue responses and interactive tools. For example, if a student selects 'Anxious,' the chatbot offers grounding techniques, rational reassurance, or quick action plans. If 'Need Study Help' is selected, it provides structured study breakdowns and focus timers.
6. Benefits
• Immediate accessibility without appointment barriers.
• Reduces stigma associated with mental health support.
• Encourages proactive emotional regulation.
• Supports academic productivity.
• Acts as a bridge to professional help when required.
7. Future Enhancements
• AI-powered mood tracking analytics.
• Personalized coping strategy recommendations.
• Integration with campus counseling services.
• Gamified wellness challenges.
• Anonymous peer support communities.
